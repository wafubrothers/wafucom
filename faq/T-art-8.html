1. AI-Driven: Breaking Through Traditional Testing Bottlenecks

Traditional airtightness testing relies on fixed thresholds and manual experience, often facing high false detection rates and frequent missed detections under complex conditions. 1.1 Dynamic Threshold Adaptive System

This system, based on CNN convolutional neural networks, automatically identifies leakage pattern features for different products. For example, in new energy battery pack testing, the detection precision improved from 0.01mL/min to 0.001mL/min, reducing false detection rates to below 0.3%.

A new energy battery manufacturer previously had a rework rate of 5.3% using traditional methods, costing about 350,000 RMB monthly. By analyzing 12 dimensions of data including pressure, flow, temperature, and humidity, the system builds a 3D leakage model. This successfully solved the micro-leakage detection challenge for a medical device company, reducing the missed detection rate from 3.2% to zero.

For this medical device manufacturer, micro-leaks in infusion devices pose life-threatening risks to patients. Previous single-sensor methods often resulted in missed detections. After applying WAFU Brothers‚Äô multi-sensor data fusion technology, these issues were eliminated, ensuring product quality and earning greater customer trust.
