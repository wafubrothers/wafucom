Electronic Blood Pressure Monitor Airtightness Testing Solution

1. Why is Airtightness Testing Necessary for Electronic Blood Pressure Monitors?

As a medical diagnostic device, the airtight performance of an electronic blood pressure monitor directly affects its measurement accuracy and reliability. Poor airtightness can lead to the following issues:

Issue Consequence
  • Increased measurement error
 Leakage causes pressure drop, leading to lower blood pressure readings
  • Shortened device lifespan
 Continuous leakage overworks the air pump
  • Safety risks
 Severe leakage may cause abnormal cuff inflation
  • Non-compliance with medical standards
 Fails certification standards such as YY 0670 and ISO 81060
Figure 1: Structural Diagram of Electronic Blood Pressure Monitor

A complete airtightness testing system ensures that every unit meets the medical accuracy requirements before leaving the factory. If you need a custom testing solution for your product, our medical testing specialists can provide expert consultation.
