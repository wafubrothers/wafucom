Development History

WAFU Brothers (Shenzhen) Automation Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 2009. For 16 years, it has been dedicated to the R&D and production of high-precision leak testing equipment, waterproof testers, sealing inspection instruments, and leak detectors, serving over 8,000 clients worldwide.

2009

Company Founded
