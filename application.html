Industry Solutions

We offer customized solutions and technical support for airtightness testing needs across different industries.

Automotive Industry

Providing high-precision testing for core automotive components, applied to sealing tests of engines, transmissions, fuel systems, ensuring vehicle performance and safety.

Aerospace Industry

Applied to sealing tests for spacecraft cabins and fuel systems, ensuring the safety and reliability of aerospace equipment in extreme environments.

View Solution

Medical Industry

Providing sealing tests for medical devices such as ventilators, anesthesia machines, and pharmaceutical packaging, ensuring medical safety and drug quality.

View Solution

Energy & Batteries

Packaging Industry

Testing the sealing of food packaging and vacuum packaging to extend shelf life and prevent contamination, ensuring packaging safety.
