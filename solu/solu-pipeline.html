Leak Testing in the Pipeline Industry

WAFU Brothers provides leak testers equipped with advanced pipeline leak detection technology, capable of accurately testing pipeline sealing and identifying gas leakage issues. Whether it is pressure leak testing or pipeline sealing tests, our equipment ensures safety under high-pressure conditions. Utilizing non-destructive testing (NDT) methods, we provide real-time assessment of pipeline sealing performance. Meanwhile, our automated leak testing systems improve testing efficiency, reduce accident risks, and ensure the long-term stable operation of pipeline systems.

Integrated Control Valve Water Meter

The integrated control valve water meter typically combines a mechanical metering module, an electric control valve, and a communication module. It is commonly used in: smart remote meter reading systems, intelligent buildings/campuses/industrial water management. It supports remote valve shut-off, automated meter reading, and other functions.

  • Moisture intrusion into the electronic module can corrode the circuit board, damaging communication or shut-off functions.
  • Micro-leakage in the valve body can prevent water shutoff, affecting meter reading and billing.
  • Aging seals or detached joints may cause water leakage on the user side, leading to accidents or complaints.
  • IP68 protection level is required. Leak and sealing tests must be performed before leaving the factory to verify protective capabilities.

Plastic Connector

Plastic connectors are widely used in medical devices, household appliances, electronic products, and fluid transmission pipelines, where they typically serve the purpose of transferring liquids or gases. If leak does not meet the required standards, it can pose significant risks. Therefore, professional leak testing is essential.
