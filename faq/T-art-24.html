How to Choose a Leak Tester ? 5 Key Parameters That Determine Testing Accuracy

March 25, 2026 WaFubrother Leak Tester, Waterproof Testing

With the rapid development of industries such as new energy vehicles, medical devices, and consumer electronics, leak testing has become a critical process to ensure product quality. A suitable leak tester can not only help manufacturers intercept defective products with poor sealing, but also significantly reduce after-sales failure rates. However, with so many brands and models available on the market, how do you choose the right testing equipment for your products?

This article breaks down five key parameters that must be considered during selection, helping you avoid common pitfalls and accurately match your testing needs.

1. Testing Accuracy: The Core of Everything

Testing accuracy is the most critical technical indicator of a leak tester, directly determining whether tiny leaks can be detected.

Parameter Description
Sensor Accuracy The higher the sensor accuracy, the more reliable the measurement results
Detection Sensitivity Sensitivity determines the smallest detectable pressure change
Repeatability Consistency of multiple tests on the same product

Common Pitfalls

Many manufacturers only claim “high accuracy” without specifying actual values. Reliable manufacturers like WAFU Brothers clearly indicate parameters such as 0.02%FS accuracy and 2–5Pa repeatability—these are verifiable performance indicators.

Selection Recommendations

  • For high-demand scenarios such as new energy batteries and medical devices, choose accuracy of 0.05%FS or better
  • For general applications such as consumer electronics and home appliances, 0.1%FS is sufficient

2. Pressure Range and Type

Different products have vastly different pressure requirements—a plastic housing may require only a few kPa, while metal sealed components may require several hundred kPa. If the pressure range is insufficient, the equipment cannot be used; if pressure control is inaccurate, test conditions become unreliable.

Test Requirement Recommended Pressure Range Description
Vacuum / Negative Pressure Test -100KPa ~ 0 Suitable for sealed cavities, packaging bags, etc.
Low Positive Pressure Test 0 ~ 300KPa Common for consumer electronics and plastic parts
Medium & High Pressure Test 300KPa ~ 3000KPa Metal housings and high-pressure components

Selection Recommendations

  • Select based on the actual working pressure of the product, leaving a 20% margin
  • If testing multiple products, prioritize wide-range equipment
  • If uncertain, choose a combined positive/negative pressure model for maximum flexibility

3. Testing Speed and Cycle Time

For batch testing on production lines, testing speed directly affects productivity.

Stage Time Proportion Optimization Potential
Pressurization Adjustable Choose faster filling equipment and optimize fixture design
Stabilization Depends on product volume Smaller volume leads to shorter time
Testing Depends on accuracy requirements Higher accuracy requires more time
Exhaust Adjustable Fast exhaust valves can reduce time

Selection Recommendations

  • Single-station production line: control cycle time within 15–30 seconds
  • Multi-station parallel: choose dual-station or four-station equipment to significantly improve efficiency
  • WAFU Brothers multi-channel leak tester , supports simultaneous testing of different products, greatly improving efficiency

4. Fixture Compatibility and Customization Capability

Fixtures are the key bridge connecting the tester and the product, directly affecting testing reliability and operational convenience.

Consideration Description
Filling Design Good filling design reduces pressurization time and improves efficiency
Sealing Method End-face, inner hole, or outer diameter sealing should be selected based on product structure
Clamping Method Manual, pneumatic, or automatic depending on cycle requirements
Changeover Time Time cost for switching between different products

Selection Recommendations

  • Choose manufacturers with customization capabilities rather than only standard machines
  • Request fixture design drawings and sealing material solutions
  • WAFU Brothers provides fully customized fixture services, designing contour-matching fixtures based on 3D drawings to ensure reliable sealing

5. Data Traceability and Digital Integration

Function Purpose
Data Storage Store test results locally with export capability
Communication Interfaces RS485, USB, Ethernet for MES integration
OK/NG Judgment Automatic result output with audio/visual alarms
Permission Management Prevent unauthorized parameter modification
History Query Trace records by time, batch, or product

Selection Recommendations

  • For automotive and medical industries with strict audits, ensure the equipment supports data export and traceability
  • If integration with automated production lines is required, confirm support for I/O signal interaction
  • WAFU Brothers leak testers support 100,000 data records with USB export to Excel

6. Conclusion

When selecting a leak tester, focus on testing accuracy, pressure range, testing speed, fixture compatibility, and data traceability. Only by combining these with your actual product requirements can you choose the most suitable equipment.
