Energy Battery Industry Testing Case

  1. Home

  5. Energy Battery Industry

Wafu Brothers plays a vital role in airtightness testing in the energy battery sector, providing high-precision testing equipment. Its technology is widely applied to lithium battery sealing inspection, ensuring the airtightness of battery casings and joints; used for fuel cell airtightness testing, preventing gas leaks; and also supports battery pack encapsulation inspection and power battery testing, ensuring the safe operation of electric vehicles and energy storage systems. Wafu Brothers offers reliable solutions with advanced technology, becoming an important partner for battery manufacturers.

Cylindrical Lithium-ion Battery

Safety risks: air and moisture ingress may cause internal chemical reactions leading to swelling, leakage, short circuit, or even fire and explosion.

Performance degradation: micro leaks reduce battery capacity, increase internal resistance, and affect lifespan and charge/discharge performance.

Quality instability: poor sealing leads to lower yield rates, higher repair rates, and affects shipment stability.

Lithium Polymer Battery

Ensures safety: prevents electrolyte leakage and external moisture ingress, avoiding swelling, combustion, and other risks.

Improves quality: ensures stable battery capacity and longer lifespan, reducing after-sales issues.

Process control: verifies sealing processes such as heat sealing and welding to improve yield.

Meets high standards: complies with the strict sealing requirements of high-end applications like drones and medical devices.

Ternary Lithium Battery

Higher fire risk: ternary systems are highly sensitive to moisture and electrolyte; even minor leaks can cause thermal runaway or explosion.

Shortened lifespan: insufficient airtightness leads to electrolyte evaporation and internal corrosion, causing rapid capacity decay.

Process control issues: production fluctuations may cause poor welding and sealing of caps, requiring real-time airtightness monitoring.
